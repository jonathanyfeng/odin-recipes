Seafood Fettuccine

Description: Delicious seafood Alfredo recipe with a creamy Parmesan sauce, shrimp, scallops, and fettuccine pasta.

Ingredients:

  • 1 (16 ounce) package dry fettuccine noodles
  • 1 Â½ tablespoons butter or margarine
  • 1 cup chopped green onions
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound medium shrimp - peeled and deveined
  • 1 pound sea scallops
  • 2 cups half-and-half cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch (Optional)

Steps:

  1. Cook fettuccine in lightly salted boiling water until tender but firm, about 8 minutes. Drain and reserve 1/2 cup of the pasta water.
  2. Heat butter and olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add minced garlic and shrimp. Cook until the shrimp turn pink, then remove and set aside.
  3. Pour heavy cream into the skillet and heat gently. Stir in seasonings such as salt, pepper, and optional red pepper flakes.
  4. Add the cooked shrimp back to the skillet along with additional seafood (like scallops, if desired). Cook until heated through.
  5. Serve the sauce over the cooked fettuccine. Toss to combine, adding reserved pasta water if needed for consistency.
