One-Pot Chicken Pomodoro

Description: A simple, flavorful one-pot chicken pasta dish featuring tender chicken, fresh tomatoes, and aromatic garlic.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 (16-ounce) package linguine
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

Steps:

  1. Season chicken with salt and pepper. Heat olive oil in a large skillet and brown the chicken on both sides. Remove and set aside.
  2. Add garlic to the skillet and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Pour in crushed tomatoes, chicken broth, basil, and red pepper flakes. Stir to combine.
  4. Place linguine into the skillet, followed by the browned chicken. Simmer until the pasta is cooked and the chicken is tender.
  5. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese as a garnish.
