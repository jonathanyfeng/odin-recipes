The Best Teriyaki Ribs

Description: Mouthwatering ribs marinated in a sweet and savory teriyaki sauce, slow-cooked to perfection.

Ingredients:

  • 2 racks of baby back ribs
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup pineapple juice
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup water

Steps:

  1. Prepare the ribs by removing the membrane and cutting them into sections. Place in a shallow dish.
  2. Mix soy sauce, brown sugar, vinegar, garlic, ginger, honey, and pineapple juice to create the marinade. Pour over the ribs and marinate for at least 2 hours.
  3. Preheat the oven to 325°F (165°C). Transfer ribs to a baking dish and cover with foil. Bake for 2 hours until tender.
  4. In a saucepan, heat the reserved marinade. Mix cornstarch with water and add to the marinade to thicken.
  5. Brush thickened sauce over the ribs and broil for a few minutes to caramelize. Serve hot.
